## SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

### Why STEM?
Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) instills specialized Science and Mathematics concepts, supported by technology integration and blended learning, combining conventional classroom teaching with interactive online learning.

### Why HUMSS?
Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) develops creativity, critical reasoning, and communication skills, crafted for students passionate about history, society, and civilization.

### Why ABM?
Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) equips students with Entrepreneurship, Business Management, and Marketing skills, preparing them for Accountancy or Business-related college degrees.

### Why GAS?
General Academic Strand (GAS) offers opportunities for students unsure of their college path, covering Social Science, Humanities, and Science subjects to foster holistic development and discover passions.

## JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL
FEU High School extends #FEUtureFORWARD education to incoming junior high school students through the JHS Program, where students can:

- **Learn to code**: Equip skills essential for a technology-driven society while studying junior high school.
- **Learn with support**: Receive academic and well-being support via mental health and self-help programs.

## Why Coding?
FEU High School sharpens innate technology skills by integrating coding into the JHS curriculum. **CODING** is assigning code for classification or identification and writing computer programs, crafted for younger generations to become innovators of the 4th Industrial Revolution. Students learn decomposition, pattern recognition, abstraction, and algorithms to express ideas digitally, vital for a tech-driven civilization.
