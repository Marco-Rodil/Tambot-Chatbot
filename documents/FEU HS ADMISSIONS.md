### Key Points
- FEU High School offers Junior High School (Grades 7-10) and Senior High School strands including STEM, ABM, HUMSS, and GAS.
- Application procedures for Junior and Senior High School involve preparing documents, uploading them, making payments, and receiving a Certificate of Registration.
- Slot reservations are available for a non-refundable P5,000 fee, credited to tuition, with options for on-campus, bank, or DragonPay payments.
- Discounts include 20% for PWDs, alumni, and specific conditions, with a P5,000 sibling discount, and various scholarships like AA Gold and Sarimanok.
- Payment methods include BPI bank deposits, online options like DragonPay, and credit card payments, with a 5% discount for full payments before the first day of classes.

### Offered Programs
FEU High School provides education for Junior High School (Grades 7-10) and Senior High School with strands like STEM ([Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/science-technology-engineering-and-mathematics/)), ABM ([Accounting, Business and Management](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/accountancy-business-and-management/)), HUMSS ([Humanities and Social Sciences](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/humanities-and-social-sciences/)), and GAS ([General Academic Strand](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/general-academic-strand/)), with an enhanced GAS curriculum available [here](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/feu-high-school-launches-its-new-gas-curriculum-for-s-y-2020-2021/).

### Application Procedures
For both Junior and Senior High School, the process includes:
- **Prepare Documents**: Scanned copies in PDF format, such as report cards, Certificates of Candidacy, Good Moral Character, and PSA Birth Certificate. For Junior High, specific grade report cards are required; for Senior High, Grade 10 report cards or completion certificates are needed, with optional DepEd vouchers.
- **Upload Documents**: Use the respective forms, like [this one for Junior High](https://forms.gle/LxBJw4ePe29C2fCP7) or [this for Senior High](https://forms.gle/Ywzv4M2QTyyv8ZycA), collecting parent email for the Canvas app.
- **Payment**: Down payment of P17,000 or P12,000 with reservation, via DragonPay ([learn more](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/other-payment-options/)) or BPI bank deposit (Account Name: FEU High School, Inc., Account Number: 1581 0021 29). A 5% discount is available for full payments before the first day, contact [finance@feuhighschool.edu.ph](mailto:finance@feuhighschool.edu.ph) for details.
- **Certificate of Registration**: Check registered email for COR, request tuition fees [here](https://forms.office.com/r/WFiHHiCwip).

Grade 12 transferees follow a similar process, starting with an [enrollment form](https://forms.gle/Za1v72Lw9M2bxXRCA), with a P17,000 down payment via DragonPay ([quarterly](http://bit.ly/DPQuarterly), [semestral](http://bit.ly/DPSemestral)) or BPI.

### Slot Reservations
Students can reserve a slot with a P5,000 non-refundable fee, credited to tuition, through on-campus visits, BPI deposits (Account Name: FEU HIGH SCHOOL, INC., Account Number: 1581-0021-29, Branch: FEU-Morayta), or DragonPay ([payment options](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/other-payment-options/)). For bank or DragonPay, send payment slip to [jcalma@feuhighschool.edu.ph](mailto:jcalma@feuhighschool.edu.ph) with details. Contact RR Laureano (0926 395 8086), Jinelyn Salameda (0966 729 1810), or Jencille Ibloguin (0945 840 2488) for assistance. Note, Grade 12 students need not reserve.

### Discounts and Scholarships
Discounts include:
- 20% for PWDs with valid ID or certification, requiring a PWD Application Form and medical certification.
- 20% for alumni children/siblings/grandchildren, for incoming Grades 7-11, needing Alumni ID and proof of graduation, with GWA ≥85% and no failing grades.
- P5,000 for the youngest sibling, requiring COR and proof of consanguinity.
- PD 577 for dependents of military personnel, needing an endorsement letter.

Scholarships include AA Gold (full tuition and fees for top 1-2 ranks, GWA ≥90), AA Green (full tuition discount for top 5%, GWA ≥85), Sarimanok Gold and Green (full tuition/fees for FEUHSAT excellence, GWA ≥85), and Tuition Assistance Program (50%/25% discount for family income ≤P400,000, GWA ≥85). Applications require forms, certificates, and sometimes essays, processed first-come, first-served with limited slots.

#### Application Procedures for Junior High School
The application process for Junior High School involves four steps:
- **Step 1: Prepare Documents**  
  Prepare scanned copies in PDF format for uploading:
  - Admissions Control Number (AdCon), found on the acceptance letter, apply if not admitted [here](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/application-for-admissions-to-grade-7/).
  - Certificate of Candidacy for Completion, issued by the School Principal or Registrar.
  - Scanned copy or photo of recently issued Report Card:
    - Grade 7: Upload Grade 6 report card.
    - Grade 8: Upload Grade 7 report card.
    - Grade 9: Upload Grade 8 report card.
    - Grade 10: Upload Grade 9 report card.
  - Certificate of Good Moral Character.
  - Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) issued Birth Certificate.
  If documents are incomplete, applicants may enroll conditionally by completing a Pending Requirement Agreement Form, downloadable [here](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/FEUHS-Pending-Requirement-Agreement-Form-S.Y.-24-25.docx).

- **Step 2: Upload Documents**  
  Use the FEU High School Enrollment Requirements Submissions Form for Incoming Junior High students, accessible [here](https://forms.gle/LxBJw4ePe29C2fCP7), collecting parent email addresses for the Canvas parent app.

- **Step 3: Payment**  
  Pay a down payment of P17,000 or P12,000 (with reservation fee) via:
  - **Dragonpay**: Online banking, OTC, Bayad Center, money remittances, etc., learn more [here](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/other-payment-options/). Use links for [Down Payment](https://bit.ly/3miguTU) or [Down Payment (w/ Reservation)](https://bit.ly/3mhewTU), with AdCon as the invoice number.
  - **BPI Bank Deposit/Transfer**: Account Name: FEU High School, Inc., Account Number: 1581 0021 29. Submit proof of transaction [here](https://bit.ly/3rCGOZU).
  A 5% discount is available for full payments before the first day, contact [finance@feuhighschool.edu.ph](mailto:finance@feuhighschool.edu.ph) for fee computation.

- **Step 4: Certificate of Registration**  
  Regularly check registered email for the Certificate of Registration (COR) from the Registrar’s Office, request tuition fees [here](https://forms.office.com/r/WFiHHiCwip).

Contact for queries: [admissions@feuhighschool.edu.ph](mailto:admissions@feuhighschool.edu.ph) for registration, [finance@feuhighschool.edu.ph](mailto:finance@feuhighschool.edu.ph) for fees, tuition details [here](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/tuition-fees-ay-2023-2024/).

Contact details remain [admissions@feuhighschool.edu.ph](mailto:admissions@feuhighschool.edu.ph) and [finance@feuhighschool.edu.ph](mailto:finance@feuhighschool.edu.ph), tuition [here](https://www.feuhighschool.edu.ph/tuition-fees-ay-2023-2024/).

#### Discounts (Financial Assistance)
- **PWD Discount**: 20% on tuition for persons with disabilities, requiring valid ID, PWD Application Form, and medical certification.
- **Alumni Discount**: 20% for children/siblings/grandchildren of FEU alumni, for Grades 7-11, needing Alumni ID, proof of graduation, GWA ≥85%, no failing grades, no major offenses.
- **Sibling Discount**: P5,000 for youngest sibling, requiring COR, school IDs, proof of consanguinity.
- **PD 577 Discount**: Exemption for dependents of military personnel, needing endorsement letter.
Reminders: First-come, first-served, limited slots, complete requirements processed immediately.
